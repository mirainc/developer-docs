A Whole Foods Market inspired menu powered by our On-Brand Menu API, built with NextJS and hosted on Vercel.

![Whole Foods Market Preview](/docs-static/whole-foods-menu-preview.png)

## Repository

[https://github.com/mirainc/wholefoods-menu-app](https://github.com/mirainc/wholefoods-menu-app)

## URL Template

```bash
https://wholefoods-menu-raydiant-app.vercel.app/:menuboard?menu=:menu
```

[Preview](https://wholefoods-menu-raydiant-app.vercel.app/menu-boards/juices?menu=5ffd5ad4-d853-48cd-a19b-421490314676)
